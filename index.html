About Us

Write It. Lock It. Share It.

At Trust Note, we believe your information deserves complete protection. Our platform allows you to share text files safely through password-locked access, ensuring only authorized users can read your content. Designed with privacy at its core, Trust Note provides a reliable, user-friendly solution for secure communication without unnecessary complexity.

How It Works

Step: 01

Upload Note

Add your text by typing or pasting it into the note box — quick and effortless.

Step: 02

Set a Password

Create a secure password to lock your note and protect your information.

Step: 03

Share the Link

Get a unique, encrypted link you can send to anyone you trust.

Step: 04

Unlock & View

The receiver enters the correct password and instantly reads your secure note.

Step: 05

Set Expiry Time

Choose how long your shared note stays active — auto-delete enhances privacy.

Step: 06

Track Access

Know when your note was opened for better control and transparency.
